Nvidia Memory and Storage Timeline

Can AI computing draw inspiration for Quantum computing for having long fiber‑optic loops as a cache memory, which could continuously stream neural‑network weights into an DRAM like cache for deterministic AI workloads. This approach would dramatically cut DRAM power use and echoes historic delay‑line memories, but faces big practical obstacles such as the huge length of fiber required, cost, and signal‑amplification overhead. Nearer‑term alternative: large banks of flash chips wired directly to AI accelerators via a new standardized High Bandwidth Interconnect.
